The Growth Path

Three pillars. One sustainable career.

Your career isn't just about teaching better. It's about building better — across teaching, business, and mindset.

🧗

Teaching

Your craft. Linear progression — you can't shortcut experience. Earn your stripes on the mat.

  • Newly Qualified Teacher
  • Yoga Teacher
  • Experienced Teacher (4yrs / 2,000hrs)
  • Senior Teacher (8yrs / 4,000hrs)

Linear — cannot be shortcut. Hours counted from qualification.

📈

Business

Your livelihood. Non-linear — if you have the skills, you can leapfrog. Most start trading time for money:

  • Self-Employed (time for money)
  • Personal Brand (you're no longer the product)
  • Business Owner (multiple streams, team)

Non-linear — a new teacher with business skills can jump straight to Brand or Business.

💡

Wellbeing & Mindset

The unlock. Without this shift, the other pillars collapse. Most teachers are stuck here:

  • Scarcity ("I shouldn't charge")
  • Awareness ("something must change")
  • Abundance (operating above the line)

The foundation everything else is built on.
